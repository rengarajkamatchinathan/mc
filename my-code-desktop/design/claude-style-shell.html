Finding Audit Logs and Cost Details in Azure

Based on your screenshot, here are the ways to see audit logs and track costs:

1. Azure Activity Log (For operations/requests)

  • In the left sidebar, go to Activity log (under Monitoring)
  • Shows who did what, when, and at what resource level
  • Filter by:
    • Time range
    • Resource (your services)
    • Operation type
    • Status

2. Cost Management + Cost Analysis (Current view)

To drill deeper into costs:

  • Click on individual services (like "Foundry models")
  • Use the Download button to export detailed cost data
  • Group by Resource, Service name, or Tag to see where spend lands
